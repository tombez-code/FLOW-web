A Day at FLOW

Every day is a new adventure where different activities, collaboration, and time for play and rest blend together. We constantly look for new ways to connect individual work with collective experiences, creating space for discovery and growth.

School opens its doors at 7:30, when morning care begins, lasting until 8:15. Children should arrive at school by 8:10 at the latest, as we start the day at 8:15 with a morning circle. This circle helps us align our plans, settle in, and gives children space for mutual communication, which they greatly need. This is followed by two morning 90-minute blocks. Lunch is scheduled at 12 noon, after which the afternoon programme and after-school clubs begin, with the option of care until 5:00 PM.
