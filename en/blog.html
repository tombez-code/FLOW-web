Why I founded Flow School and how I see education

In 2013, my boys Théodore and Louis were born. Although they are twins, they couldn't be more different. While Louis loves maths and could calculate from morning to evening, Théodore is a creative soul, always drawing and making things. For a long time I wondered where they would study one day. I was looking for a school that could develop their talents, but also their emotional intelligence and empathy.
