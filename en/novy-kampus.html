FAQ

Location: Strategic and safe

We were not just looking for a building, we were looking for accessibility. Our new home in Balabence is located at the strategic intersection of Prague 8 and 9, which means one thing – less time in traffic and easier logistics for the family.

  • Address: Českomoravská 1a, 190 00 Prague 9.
  • Transport hub: We are just a short distance from Českomoravská metro station (line B) and directly at tram stops (lines 1, 2, 3, 5, 6 and others).
  • Possibility to park on a side street or at nearby Harfa Gallery. At the same time, we plan a kiss-and-ride zone right in front of the building and for maximum comfort we are finalizing cooperation with Liftago for shuttle service option.
  • Green space and sports: We are surrounded by parks and just a 5-minute walk from a modern sports arena.

Campus: Space created for concentration

The new building is not just a school, it's an ecosystem. Na more than 1 600 m² we designed an environment that supports "flow" in learning and rest. Safety and hygiene are paramount – we haveown entrance separated from office tenants and modern ventilation with heat recovery.

  • Small groups: Maximum 16 children per classroom for individual approach.
  • Top-class facilities: Up to 18 core classes, specialized classrooms, "Deep work" zones for deep concentration and relaxation corners.
  • Little FLOW: Includes a new kindergarten school for 24 children with its own entrance and schedule (start September 2026).
  • Comfort for parents and children: Own dining hall with regard to diets, changing rooms and unique coworking zone directly for parents.

FAQ

Building, facilities and safety

Is the building reserved exclusively for the school, or is it shared with other tenants?

The office building has its own entrance, FLOW also has its own. Everything is approved by the hygiene authority and fire department (emergency routes, drills, compliance with regulations).

Who else will be in the building and how is the operation separated?

There are different entrances and separate operations. We share the floor with a small company that has fewer than 8 people.

How is student movement controlled inside (stairs, elevators, supervision)?

Stairs and elevator are available with access for people with special needs.

Are there any risks in the area (busy roads, industrial zones, construction)?

No

Do the classrooms have enough daylight? Are there rooms without windows?

Yes, they do. We will soon provide additional visualizations and invite you to tour the spaces.

Noise: noise from traffic, neighbors, acoustics in corridors/in classrooms?

All noise will be monitored by the health authority, limit is below 45 dB.

Air quality: ventilation/heat recovery? CO2 monitoring?

It is a modern building with full ventilation and heat recovery system.

Lockers/changing rooms: Will this be organized?

Yes

Calm/sensory space for children who need a break (wellbeing room, counselor)?

Yes, each class has zones for deep work and relaxation. Also meeting rooms for staff and meetings with parents.

Transport and logistics

Parking / drop off / pick up?

Yes, you can use the surrounding streets. We are also exploring the possibility of using a garage in the building (to be specified). We will also apply for a Kiss & Ride zone in front of the building.

Will we offer shuttle service?

Yes, we are concluding an agreement with Liftago, more information coming soon.

What is the procedure for child handover (check-in/check-out)?

we have our own reception and entrance secured by chips.

Meals, sports and leisure

Lunch at school / catering?

Yes, external suppliers and our own kitchen. Includes all dietary needs, allergies, gluten-free meals, vegetarian etc.

Drinking water: water station and easy access during the day?

Yes

School gymnasium and sports?

Yes, a modern gym/sports arena is a 5-minute walk from the school campus.

Outdoor space: where children spend time outside (courtyard/park/playground)?

Yes, we are surrounded by parks and playgrounds. We would also like to explore the possibility of renting part of the grounds from a nearby public school.

After-school club and clubs: lessons, capacity, spaces in the new campus?

This will be prepared during the summer holidays. As for quality, we will do everything to keep our promise.

Teaching organisation and administration

Is this a long-term location (no moving in 1-2 years)?

Yes, we have a 10-year contract with the option to extend for another 5 years, repeatedly.

Will the teaching team remain stable during the move?

Yes, we introduced the new building to the team and received very positive feedback.

Will the class size or capacity change?

Yes, the new limit is 16 children per class.

Any new fees (equipment fee, deposits, transportation fees)?

No. Just for transportation / shuttle service. It will be handled individually.

What if the moving schedule shifts – what guarantees exist for families?

We will do everything to prevent this. But in that case we would extend the lease in the current building by 2-3 months.

Will families be asked to sign new contracts/amendments?

Probably an amendment covering the new location + new school rules / regulations.

Little FLOW kindergarten: start date, capacity, separate entrance, daily schedule?

September 2026, 24 children, separate entrance, own operation. Everything will be presented soon.

Communication and next steps

Who is the main contact person (so questions don't get lost)?

Use the new email address campus@skolaflow.cz + ask Marva.

Will there be tours for parents and children? When and how many?

Yes, the date will be specified (TBD).

How will you handle rumors/misinformation in WhatsApp groups of parents?

Please, in case of doubts contact Marva directly at 777858922 – send an SMS with your name and question.
