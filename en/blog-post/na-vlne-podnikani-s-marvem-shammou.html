For Parents

Are you looking for a place where learning meets joy for your child?

For the 2026/27 school year we are accepting pupils at both levels of our primary school and are opening our arms to the youngest in our new nursery LittleFLOW.

Open Day — Wednesday 6. 5. at 17:00

Register

We don't know exactly what to teach children because we don't know what the world will look like when they finish school. Most importantly, the ability to adapt to change and resilience to stress will help them. Thanks to these, they will be able to cope with everything that comes their way and will be able to face it as a challenge, says Marv Shamma in a new episode of the On the Wave of Entrepreneurship podcast, founder of Flow primary school built on a holistic approach. How did the coach and entrepreneur move from adult education to children's education? Through his own.

The guest for the next continuation of the On the Wave of Entrepreneurship podcast was founder and director of Flow School Marv Shamma.
