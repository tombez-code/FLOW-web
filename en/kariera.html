Our vision

Our vision is a happy, fulfilled person truly prepared for life in the 21st century. Preparedness in our view means the ability to quickly adapt to a changing world full of new challenges. Our shared goal is to prepare children for the following: "I may not know what awaits me in the future, but I have the mindset to overcome even the unknown."

How do we do it?

The school currently covers grades 1–5 and will naturally grow into a full primary school. Each class can accommodate up to 24 students.

Each class is led by a qualified Czech teacher, a teaching assistant, and a native English speaker. We have a support team of specialists for both teachers and children, including a child psychologist, educational methodologist, and speech therapist. We also collaborate with external experts to connect learning with the real world.

What do we offer?

You will be part of a big vision, not just as an educator but as a partner. We offer a solid salary, an open team culture, and fair financial compensation commensurate with your experience and ambition to grow.

  • Internal training in methodology, leadership, psychology and talent development
  • Study leave and financial contribution for professional development
  • Sick days
  • Tuition discount for your children
