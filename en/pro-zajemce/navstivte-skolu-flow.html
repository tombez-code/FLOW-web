Good to know

By prior arrangement only: book a time in advance and bring your ID with you.

After booking: you will receive an email with confirmation and details of your meeting. If you need to reschedule, použijte odkaz v tomto e-mailu nebo nás kontaktujte na adrese info@skolaflow.cz

Photography: due to student privacy protection, photography is not allowed during lessons.

If your preferred time is not available, send an email to info@skolaflow.cz and we will offer you alternatives.
